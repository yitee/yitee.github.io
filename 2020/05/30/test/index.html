Do you have a dream?It’s the kind of thing grows in your blood,or something as obsessive as love.Before the dream comes true, it will always test you in unexpected ways. Frustrations, failures, confusions, abandon. Dream often takes one year, three years or even ten years to test us if we truly love it, if we have persisted without giving up, if we deserve to be rewarded. Dream is beautiful and powerful, attracting us to fight for it or take risks even give up everything for it. But now I realize it is not dream but who is chasing it is beautiful and powerful.

你有梦想吗，就是那种向命一样的东西，或是像爱情一样让人痴迷的事情。梦想实现之前，它总会以意想不到的方式考验你。沮丧、失败、迷茫、放弃。时常一年、三年甚至于十年来考验我们是否真的爱它，是否坚持从未放弃，是否值得被它眷顾。梦想好像闪着光，吸引着我们为之奋斗或者冒险甚至为之付出一切。但其实梦想本不会发光，发光的是追梦的你。
